# Telegram Config Spec

## Animation Concept

- **Idle:** A gentle blue wave representing signal transmission.
- **Interaction:** A paper plane flying across the section header.
- **Success:** The paper plane lands smoothly.
- **Error:** The paper plane stalls and turns red.

## Color Palette

- **Primary:** #0088cc (Curses: BLUE)
- **Secondary:** #FFFFFF (Curses: WHITE)
- **Background:** Default terminal background

## ASCII Assets

- Paper plane: `>-`
- Wave: `~~~`
